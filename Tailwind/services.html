Our Services

Comprehensive healthcare solutions to address your medical needs with expertise and compassion

Cardiology Services

Cardiac Consultation

Comprehensive evaluation by our experienced cardiologists to assess heart health and provide personalized treatment plans.

  • Thorough medical history review
  • Physical examination
  • Risk assessment for heart disease
  • Treatment recommendations

Echocardiography

Non-invasive ultrasound procedure to evaluate heart structure and function by creating detailed images.

  • Assesses heart chambers and valves
  • Evaluates pumping function
  • Detects abnormalities
  • Monitors treatment progress

Electrocardiogram (ECG)

Records electrical activity of the heart to detect irregular heartbeats, heart attacks, and other cardiac issues.

  • Quick and painless procedure
  • Detects arrhythmias
  • Identifies signs of heart damage
  • Monitors pacemaker function

Cardiac Rehabilitation

Supervised program to help patients recover from heart attacks, surgery, and other cardiac conditions.

  • Exercise training
  • Education on heart-healthy living
  • Stress reduction techniques
  • Nutritional counseling

Spine Care

Fracture Care

  • Long-term follow-up care
