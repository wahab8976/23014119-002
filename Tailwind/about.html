Learn about our mission, values, and commitment to providing exceptional healthcare

Our Story

Founded in 1995, MediCare Hospital began as a small community clinic with a vision to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare to all. Over the years, we have grown into a comprehensive medical center serving thousands of patients annually, while maintaining our commitment to compassionate care.

Our state-of-the-art facility spans over 250,000 square feet and houses the latest medical technology and equipment. We have expanded our services to include specialized departments in cardiology, orthopedics, pediatrics, neurology, oncology, and many more.

What sets us apart is our team of over 200 dedicated healthcare professionals who are committed to our patient-centered approach. We believe in treating the whole person, not just the illness, and this philosophy guides every aspect of our care.

Today, MediCare Hospital stands as a beacon of healthcare excellence in the region, continuously evolving to meet the changing needs of our community while staying true to our founding principles of compassion, integrity, and excellence.
