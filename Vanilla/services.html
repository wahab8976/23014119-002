Our Medical Services

Comprehensive healthcare solutions for all your needs

Comprehensive Healthcare

MediCare Hospital offers a wide range of medical services delivered by experienced healthcare professionals using state-of-the-art technology.

Specialty Care Services

Our hospital offers specialized medical services across various departments, each staffed with expert physicians and healthcare professionals.

Cardiology

Comprehensive care for heart conditions including diagnostic tests, interventional procedures, and rehabilitation services. Our cardiologists specialize in treating various heart diseases including coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmias, and valvular heart disease.

  • Echocardiography
  • Cardiac Catheterization
  • Electrocardiogram (ECG)
  • Holter Monitoring
  • Cardiac Rehabilitation

Orthopedics

Our orthopedic department offers comprehensive care for musculoskeletal conditions affecting bones, joints, muscles, ligaments, and tendons. We provide both surgical and non-surgical treatments for acute injuries and chronic conditions.

  • Joint Replacement Surgery
  • Sports Medicine
  • Trauma Care
  • Spine Surgery
  • Physical Therapy

Pediatrics

Our pediatric department provides comprehensive healthcare services for infants, children, and adolescents. Our child-friendly environment and specialized pediatricians ensure the best care for your children's health and development.

  • Well-child Check-ups
  • Immunizations
  • Treatment for Childhood Illnesses
  • Developmental Assessments
  • Pediatric Emergency Care

Neurology

Our neurology department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves. Our neurologists utilize advanced diagnostic technologies to provide precise diagnosis and effective treatment plans.

  • Electroencephalography (EEG)
  • Electromyography (EMG)
  • Treatment for Headaches and Migraines
  • Stroke Management
  • Epilepsy Care

Obstetrics & Gynecology

Our OB/GYN department provides comprehensive care for women at every stage of life. From routine check-ups to specialized treatments, our team of gynecologists and obstetricians ensures high-quality care for women's health needs.

  • Prenatal Care
  • Family Planning
  • Gynecological Surgery
  • Menopause Management
  • High-Risk Pregnancy Care

Oncology

Our oncology department offers comprehensive cancer care, from early detection to advanced treatment options. Our multidisciplinary team of oncologists works together to provide personalized treatment plans for various types of cancer.

  • Chemotherapy
  • Radiation Therapy
  • Surgical Oncology
  • Cancer Screening Programs
  • Supportive Care Services

Diagnostic Services

Our hospital is equipped with advanced diagnostic technologies to provide accurate and timely diagnosis for various health conditions.

Imaging Services

Our imaging department utilizes state-of-the-art technology to provide detailed images for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.

  • X-Ray
  • Computed Tomography (CT) Scan
  • Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)
  • Ultrasound
  • Mammography

Laboratory Services

Our clinical laboratory offers a wide range of diagnostic tests to help in the detection, diagnosis, and monitoring of various health conditions.

  • Blood Tests
  • Urine Analysis
  • Microbiology
  • Histopathology
  • Genetic Testing

Specialized Diagnostics

We offer specialized diagnostic procedures for complex health conditions requiring detailed examination and assessment.

  • Endoscopy
  • Colonoscopy
  • Bronchoscopy
  • Cardiac Stress Tests
  • Sleep Studies

Preventive Care Services

Our preventive care services focus on maintaining health, preventing disease, and detecting health issues early when treatment is more effective.

Health Screenings

Regular health screenings can help detect health issues before they become serious. We offer various screening programs tailored to different age groups and risk factors.

  • Annual Physical Examinations
  • Cancer Screenings
  • Cardiovascular Risk Assessment
  • Diabetes Screening
  • Bone Density Testing

Immunizations

We provide immunizations for all age groups to protect against various infectious diseases, following the recommended vaccination schedules.

  • Childhood Vaccinations
  • Adolescent Immunizations
  • Adult Vaccinations
  • Travel Immunizations
  • Seasonal Flu Shots

Health Education

Our health education programs aim to empower individuals with knowledge and skills to maintain good health and prevent diseases.

  • Nutritional Counseling
  • Weight Management Programs
  • Smoking Cessation
  • Stress Management
  • Chronic Disease Management

Emergency Services

Our emergency department is equipped to handle medical emergencies of all kinds, providing prompt and effective care when you need it most.

24/7 Emergency Care

Our emergency department is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, staffed with experienced emergency physicians and nurses ready to provide immediate care for critical conditions.

  • Trauma Care
  • Cardiac Emergencies
  • Stroke Management
  • Respiratory Emergencies
  • Pediatric Emergency Services

Urgent Care

For non-life-threatening conditions that require prompt attention, our urgent care services provide timely medical care without the wait times typically associated with emergency departments.

  • Minor Injuries
  • Fever and Flu Symptoms
  • Sprains and Strains
  • Cuts and Lacerations
  • Infections

Ambulance Services

Our ambulance services provide rapid response and transportation to our emergency department for patients experiencing medical emergencies.

  • Advanced Life Support
  • Basic Life Support
  • Critical Care Transport
  • Neonatal Transport
  • Inter-facility Transfers
