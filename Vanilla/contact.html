Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and facilities

How do I schedule an appointment?

+

You can schedule an appointment by calling our appointment desk at (123) 456-7890, using our contact form on this page, or visiting the hospital during regular hours. For specialist consultations, we recommend calling in advance to ensure availability.

What insurance plans do you accept?

+

MediCare Hospital accepts most major insurance plans including Medicare, Medicaid, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, Cigna, and United Healthcare. We recommend contacting your insurance provider to verify coverage before your appointment or procedure.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

+

For your first appointment, please bring your photo ID, insurance card, list of current medications, medical records or referrals from other healthcare providers, and any relevant imaging or test results. It's also helpful to arrive 15 minutes early to complete registration forms.

Do you offer emergency services?

+

Yes, our emergency department is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. For life-threatening emergencies, please call 911 immediately. Our emergency team is equipped to handle a wide range of urgent medical conditions.

Are there visiting hours for inpatients?

+

General visiting hours are from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily. Specialized units like ICU may have more restricted visiting hours. We recommend checking with the specific department or nursing station for the most accurate information.

How can I access my medical records?

+

Patients can request access to their medical records by submitting a written request to our Health Information Management department. You can also contact us at (123) 456-7890 ext. 4567 for assistance with obtaining your medical records.
