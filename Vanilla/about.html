About MediCare Hospital

Excellence in healthcare since 1995

Our Story

MediCare Hospital was founded in 1995 with a vision to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare services to the community. What began as a small clinic with a handful of dedicated physicians has grown into a comprehensive medical center offering a wide range of specialized services.

Over the years, we have continuously evolved to meet the changing healthcare needs of our patients, investing in advanced medical technology and expanding our team of healthcare professionals. Today, MediCare Hospital stands as a testament to our unwavering commitment to excellence in patient care.

Throughout our journey, our focus has remained on treating the whole person—not just the illness—and creating a healing environment where patients feel valued, respected, and cared for with compassion.

Our Facilities

State-of-the-art infrastructure designed for comfort and care

Modern Patient Rooms

Our private and semi-private patient rooms are designed to provide comfort, privacy, and a healing environment. Each room is equipped with modern amenities and advanced monitoring systems to ensure patient safety and comfort.

Advanced Operation Theaters

Our operation theaters are equipped with cutting-edge technology and adhere to the highest standards of safety and sterility. The integrated systems allow for precision surgical procedures and improved patient outcomes.

Diagnostic Imaging Center

Our comprehensive diagnostic imaging center features state-of-the-art equipment including MRI, CT scan, ultrasound, and X-ray machines. These advanced technologies enable accurate diagnosis and effective treatment planning.

Rehabilitation Center

Our rehabilitation center provides comprehensive physical, occupational, and speech therapy services. The center is equipped with specialized equipment and staffed by experienced therapists to support patients in their recovery journey.
